BANGTAN SONYEONDAN

As known as BTS.

Bangtan Sonyeondan is a South Korean boyband. BTS, also known as the Bangtan Boys, that has gained immense popularity both in South Korea and internationally. The group consists of seven members: RM (formerly known as Rap Monster), Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. BTS is known for their catchy music, impressive choreography, and meaningful lyrics that often touch on themes such as mental health, self-love, and social issues. They have a massive global fanbase and have achieved numerous records and accolades in the music industry.
